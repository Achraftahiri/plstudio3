About Us

We are a dedicated team of developers, designers, artists, programmers, and most importantly gaming enthusiasts. Every day our goal is to immerse you in high-quality digital experiences that transport you, your family and friends into new worlds. We’re a studio that loves to always try new things, learn from what we do wrong, and celebrate the things we get right! Our team is able to create a complete video game from concept to launch as well as take part in co-productions, porting and remasters. We can create video games on almost any platform and with almost any technology, but we are specialised in 2D Games . We’re a company that can help you with whatever your burdens are as we can cover every aspect of video game development – be it programming, art, design or QA. We are currently working on our own game projects, We are trying to work with different technologies from what we already know, To improve our experience specially in the 3D games. We started to explore more platforms like Html5 , TV and Console games . We are Positive about the future of this new company, and if you are motivated to join us.
